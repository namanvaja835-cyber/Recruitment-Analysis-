Risk Factor Contribution Breakdown

Live Updates
See how each factor contributes to the overall illustrative risk score in real-time.
🚨
Sales Representatives at Highest Risk
Sales Representatives show 39.8% attrition — nearly 2.5× the company average. Targeted retention programs for this role could significantly reduce overall attrition.
⏰
Overtime is a Major Red Flag
Employees working overtime face 30.5% attrition vs. only 10.4% for those who don't — a 3× increase. Overtime policies need urgent review.
📈
Stock Options Retain Talent
Employees with no stock options show 24.4% attrition vs. just 7.6% for level-2 holders. Stock options are the #1 SHAP driver of retention.
👶
Young & New Employees Leave Fast
Ages 18-24 have 39.2% attrition. Employees in their first year have 34.9% — suggesting onboarding and mentorship gaps.
💍
Single Employees More Likely to Leave
Singles show 25.5% attrition vs. 10.1% for divorced employees — likely correlating with age and career stage mobility.
✈️
Frequent Travel Drives Attrition
Frequent travellers face 24.9% attrition vs. 8.0% for non-travellers. Work-from-home or reduced travel policies could improve retention.
